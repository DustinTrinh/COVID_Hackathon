For the People & Community

Everyone's safety is our main goal and purpose to create this application. To prevent the spread of COVID-19, it is best that everyone should stay home and isolate themselves from crowds. Though, there are some events that force people to leave their houses such as medical appointments, works or paying for bills. Our application targets mostly on the way people pay their bills nowadays. Long story short, we bring all of your bills together to one place on users can easily scan and pay from anywhere.  

For Businesses, Big or Small

A completely new way for Business owners to pay for their ultilities, rents, products and almost anything. Owners can as well ask for payment by using Pay@Ease. 


Based on our researches and interviews, it is very inconvinence for businesses owners to log on to different platforms to pay for different things. To prevent that from happening, Pay@Ease is trying to get as many users onto the platform as possible.

What We Do?

Pay@Ease simply connects Payers and Payees. One of our goal is to assemble as many Payers into our application as possible. So both sides can easily request or pay the bills within our app only.

Businesses Actions

Authentication

Payers signup/login into Pay@Ease and go through authentication process.

Information

Only need to provide the Bill Amount. Pay@Ease will take care of the rest. Such as generate QR. 

Upload

Post bills to Pay@Ease platform and display to Customer. And ... DONE
                                                            

Pay@Ease Action

1. Receive

Intake the bills to start processing.

2. Organize

Prepare and reformat bills, generate code for payment. 

3. Display

Display the bill to the customers so they can pay

Pay & Update

Update the status for the bill from both sides.

Users Actions

One

Scan the code on the bill to see details and choose payment method.

*Able to add multiple payment methods into one account*

Two

Make a payment.

Three

Receive updates from payments.
